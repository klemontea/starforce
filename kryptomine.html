Home / Kryptomine

SPACE MINING

❮ ❯

Kryptomine is not a planet for living because of its lack of water and plants. However, it has lots of minerals that don't exist on Earth. These minerals are used as materials for infrastructure, medical, vehicles, and weapon. Hence, this place is only used as mining site and its only main activity. The land consists of montains and there are only 3% of water. It is like a dry land. Weather is mostly hot and only little oxygen detected. Therefore, visitors who come must wear an astronaut suit.

OUR ACTIVITIES

Stone Digger

More

The advancement of space technology is due to the materials it is built in with. These materials are only exist in this planet, and you will see how it is extracted and processed. You will also get a chance to use one of our mining tools.

Stone Museum

More

The abundance of mineral resources in Kryptomine are not all explored yet. As we try to expand our mining coverge, it is a best idea to add each mineral in a museum so that visitor can view and study them. Currently, there are 70 types of minerals that you can view.
