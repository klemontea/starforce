CITY OF LIGHT

❮ ❯

This planet, also called The City of Light, is the most populated one among other planets. It also the only touchpoint from any incoming spaceship from Earth. It also a place where visitors could stay in hotel. It is the heart of space civilizations which could take place in future. All Star resembles like Earth and consists of 60% water and 40% land. Oxygen exists in this place, and also other geographical entities like mountain, tree, lake, and hill. As a modern city, All Star has lots of skycrapper building, places, and fun or even a new one that you can't find on Earth.
