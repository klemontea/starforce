PLANET OF THE OCEAN

❮ ❯

Covered by sea, Waterworld is a planet which has only 5% of land in total. The sea water is not so much different from Earth in terms of texture and taste, and it supports many kind of life below it. There are estimated to be around 10,000 types of fish aliens lives in the sea. Some of them are dangerous while others don't. The depth of sea varies among areas, and up until now, there is no information about how depth it is. The weather does not either too hot or cold and oxygen is exist in this planet.
